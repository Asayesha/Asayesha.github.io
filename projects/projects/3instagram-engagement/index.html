Instagram Engagement

Considering that in 2014 social users posted an average of 1.8 billion photos to the internet daily and the growth of image-centric platforms like Instagram, Snapchat and Pinterest makes analyzing visual data also very important to understand your audience. Using data scraped form the Instagram page of Natgeo, this project answers the question What should National Geographic do to increase engagement on its Instagram page?

Project link: https://github.com/Asayesha/Instagram-Engagement
